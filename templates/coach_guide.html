🎾 App Guide

🏠 Home 📋 Match Organiser

For Coaches: Quickly organize fair and balanced tennis sessions with this step-by-step guide.

✅ Overview

This app helps you organize singles or doubles matches based on player grades, track participation, and even import player lists via CSV.

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

🔑

1. Access the App

Open the app in your browser or install it on your iPad as a Progressive Web App for quick access.

⚙️

2. Set Up the Session

  • Number of Courts: Set how many courts are available.
  • Matches per Player: Set how many matches each player should play.
  • Match Format: Choose Singles or Doubles.
  • View Schedule By: Switch between viewing matches by court or by round.
👤

3. Add Players

Enter each player's Name and Grade (1 = strongest, 4 = weakest), then click "Add Player."

You can also limit how many rounds a player participates in by clicking the "Rounds" button next to their name.

📤

4. Upload a CSV (Optional)

Click "Upload CSV" to load players from a spreadsheet with name and grade columns.

🎯

5. Organize Matches

Click "Organize Matches" to generate matchups based on player grades.

Singles Example: Jamie (g1) vs. Jake (g3) - Court 3
Doubles Example: Jamie & Jake (avg g1.5) vs. Sam & Alex (avg g2) - Court 1
📊

6. Review Player Stats

  • Matches Played
  • Average Opponent Grade
  • Grade Difference Summary
🔀

7. Reshuffle Options

Use various reshuffle options to fine-tune matchups:

  • Reshuffle All: Mix up all matchups without changing settings.
  • Reshuffle Round: Reorganize just a specific round while keeping others intact.
⏱️

8. Match Timer

Use the built-in timer to keep track of match rounds.

  • Set custom duration in minutes
  • Start, pause, and reset timer functions
🔄

9. Reset All

Click "Reset All" to clear players and matchups when you want to start fresh.

💡 Pro Tips:
  • Best experienced on iPad in landscape or desktop.
  • You can limit certain players to fewer rounds if they need to leave early.
  • Organize by player grade to create balanced, competitive matches.
