🎾 App Guide

🏠 Home 📋 Match Organiser

For Coaches: Quickly organize fair and balanced tennis sessions with this step-by-step guide.

✅ Overview

This app helps you organize singles or doubles matches based on player grades, track participation, and even import player lists via CSV.

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1. Access the App

Open the app in your browser or on your iPad.

2. Set Up the Session

  • Number of Courts: Set how many courts are available.
  • Matches per Player: Set how many matches each player should play.
  • Match Format: Choose Singles or Doubles.
  • View Schedule By: Switch between viewing matches by court or by round.

3. Add Players

Enter each player’s Name and Grade (1 = strongest, 4 = weakest), then click “Add Player.”

4. Upload a CSV (Optional)

Click “Upload CSV” to load players from a spreadsheet with name and grade columns.

5. Organize Matches

Click “Organize Matches” to generate matchups.

6. Review Player Stats

  • Matches Played
  • Average Opponent Grade
  • Grade Difference Summary

7. Reshuffle

Click “Reshuffle” to mix up the matchups without changing settings.

8. Save/Load Sessions (coming soon)

  • Save with a name
  • Load previous sessions

9. Reset All

Click “Reset All” to clear players and matchups.

💡 Tips:
  • Best experienced on iPad in landscape or desktop.
  • Use the match timer to track session rounds.

🧠 Full Feature Summary

  • CoachBot – Ask tennis-related questions and get quick coaching advice.
  • AI Toolbox – Generate session plans and drills tailored to player count, focus, and duration.
  • Notes – Store and manage personal session notes.
  • Calendar – Plan, view, and repeat coaching events (players only see their coach’s events).
  • Weather Forecast – 5-day hourly breakdown with tennis suitability for your default postcode.
  • Bulletin Board – Share messages and updates with your group (each coach sees only their own).
  • Player Portal – Give access via codes to view calendar, bulletins, weather, and ask CoachBot.
  • Settings – Configure your postcode for weather forecasting.
