🎾 How to Use the Tennis Session Organizer App

For Coaches: Quickly organize fair and balanced tennis sessions with this step-by-step guide.

✅ Overview

This app helps you organize singles or doubles matches based on player grades, track participation, and even import player lists via CSV.

🗺️ Step-by-Step Guide

1. Access the App

Open the app in your browser or on your iPad.

2. Set Up the Session

  • Number of Courts: Set how many courts are available.
  • Number of Rounds: Set how many rounds of matches you'd like each player to play.
  • Match Format: Choose Singles or Doubles.

3. Add Players

Enter each player’s Name and Grade (1 to 4, 1 being strongest), then click “Add Player.”

4. Upload a CSV (Optional)

Click “Upload CSV” to load players from a spreadsheet with Name and Grade columns.

5. Organize Matches

Click “Organize Matches” to automatically generate fair matchups based on grade, court count and rounds selected.

Match format example:

Jamie (g1) vs. Jake (g3) - Court 3

For doubles, players will be paired based on their grades to ensure balanced teams. The g (grade) will be an average of the 2 players

Example:

Jamie & Jake (avg g1.5) vs. Sam & Alex (avg g2) - Court 1

6. Review Player Stats

At the bottom, check stats for each player:

  • Matches Played
  • More Stats coming soon

7. Reshuffle if Needed

Click “Reshuffle” to generate new matchups with the same players and settings.

8. Save/Load Sessions [coming soon]

  • Use the session name box to Save the current setup.
  • Load previous sessions as needed.

9. Reset All

Click “Reset All” to clear players and matchups while keeping your settings intact.

10. AI Practice Drill Generator (password protected for now)

Use AI to come up with drill ideas, input a focus area and how long you have and let the magic happen.

💡 Tips:
  • Use an iPad in landscape or the web for best results.
  • There is a handy timer at the bottom to help you time each round/session.
